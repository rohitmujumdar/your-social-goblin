System architecture of Your Social Goblin: conversations are detected as dark-pattern coins, written to HydraDB memory, and recalled across sessions to derive the game and personalize repairs.

System architecture

Your Social Goblin
runs on memory.

Conversations become coins. Coins become memories in HydraDB. The whole game is just memory, written and recalled.

The flow

Write once, recall forever

Two lanes run through one core. The write lane turns a chat into a memory; the read lane rebuilds the world from those memories on every load. HydraDB sits in the middle — the only thing that persists.

The memory layer

The two things the judges score

Both requirements fall out of one decision: nothing lives in the app — everything lives in HydraDB and is recalled.

persistent memory logic

It recalls past sessions on its own

Every coin, profile, and repair is a memory. The dashboard stores no state — it is rebuilt from a live recall each load.

  • Restart the server, a new session, and the world reassembles from memory.
  • A repaired relationship stays repaired, recalled from its resolution memory.
  • The trace panel logs every autonomous write / query.
context-aware execution

Its advice changes with what it remembers

Before suggesting a fix it recalls who the person is and what worked last time, then tailors the message.

no memory"Send a follow-up."
with recalled memory"Send a short honest line — long excuses don't land with Marc, and that worked last time."
  • Marc → short honesty · Sarah → a concrete date. Same app, different advice.
write→persist→recall→adapt→write↺
